    • About Me

    Data Passionate


    It's been almost years since I began pursuing my Bachelor's in Information Technology at York University, and I'm expected to graduate in years and months.

    What I’ve learned from my degree is just how vital the connection between business and IT is in today’s world. The unique IT BComm program at York gave me a great opportunity to expand my business knowledge alongside essential IT and computer science courses.

    However, as someone passionate about data, that wasn’t enough. That’s why I’ve expanded my knowledge in data science and data analysis to combine what I’ve learned from IT and commerce with statistical models and the power of machine learning and deep learning to extract actionable insights from raw data.

    My Story

    It All Begins With Behavior

    I believe the way we think, act, and solve daily problems generates some of the most meaningful data in our lives. These everyday choices, when seen together, form patterns that reflect how we live and adapt.

    Shaped by Where We’ve Been

    Those patterns don’t just appear out of nowhere they come from our unique backgrounds and experiences. What we’ve learned, seen, and felt shapes how we make decisions, and every decision adds to a growing story of data.

    More Than Meets the Eye

    But there's more to it. Beneath our conscious choices are hidden trends subtle signals that hint at how we might act in future situations, even ones we haven’t faced yet. This is where things get truly fascinating.

    The Power of Data Science

    That’s what drew me to data science. It’s not just about numbers it’s about discovering meaning in the mess. It’s about using data to better understand human nature, and maybe even help others make smarter choices too.
