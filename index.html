    Data Passionate


    It's been almost since I began pursuing my Bachelor's in Information Technology at York University, and I'm expected to graduate in years and months.

    What I’ve learned from my degree is just how vital the connection between business and IT is in today’s world. The unique IT BComm program at York gave me a great opportunity to expand my business knowledge alongside essential IT and computer science courses.

    However, as someone passionate about data, that wasn’t enough. That’s why I’ve expanded my knowledge in data science and data analysis to combine what I’ve learned from IT and commerce with statistical models and the power of machine learning and deep learning to extract actionable insights from raw data.

    My Story

    Everything we do is data

    Every action, thought, and decision creates data. When we step back and analyze these patterns, we uncover insights that define how we live and solve problems.

    But where does it come from?

    It comes from our experiences. Our backgrounds shape who we are, and every choice we make generates unique, valuable information.

    So that was it?

    Not quite. Beneath our actions lie hidden patterns that can predict behaviors in ways we don't even realize. Data science unlocks these deeper layers of understanding.

    But why IT Bcom?

    To turn this passion into expertise, I chose York’s IT program, blending technology with business insight to better understand human behavior in the financial world.
